Who Are We?

We are Azka, Fatima and Shumaim , three friends passionate about the beauty of northern Pakistan. Through GoNorthPakistan.com, we offer authentic, eco-friendly travel experiences that let you explore the region’s breathtaking landscapes and rich cultures, all while preserving them for future generations. From the towering peaks of the Karakoram to the lush valleys of Hunza and Swat, our journeys go beyond sightseeing – they immerse you in the heart of the north. We are committed to sustainable tourism by supporting local communities and minimizing environmental impact. Join us to explore northern Pakistan responsibly and create unforgettable memories along the way. Explore the North. Travel Sustainably. Make Lasting Memories.
